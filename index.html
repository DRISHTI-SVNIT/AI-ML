ABOUT US

MACHINE LEARNING, DEEP LEARNING AND COMPUTER VISION


Machine learning is the science of getting computers to act without being explicitly programmed. Deep Learning is a subfield of machine learning concerned with algorithms inspired by the structure and function of the brain called artificial neural networks.
In the past decade, machine learning has given us self-driving cars, practical speech recognition, effective web search, and a vastly improved understanding of the human genome. AI capabilities, as they exist today, are sufficiently advanced to enable transformative product innovation and value creation across industries: from agriculture to insurance, from healthcare to education, from transportation to construction, and beyond.

Machine learning is so pervasive today that you probably use it dozens of times a day without knowing it. Many researchers also think it is the best way to make progress towards human-level AI.

Computer Vision is defined as a field of study that seeks to develop techniques to help computers “see” and understand the content of digital images such as photographs and videos. It is a multidisciplinary field that could broadly be called a subfield of artificial intelligence and machine learning.
The goal of computer vision is to understand the content of digital images, which may involve extracting a description from the image.

Here at Drishti we love to dive deep into this world of Machine learning, Computer Vision and Data Science. Creating, training and testing various machine learning and deep learning models, organising, cleaning and simplifying huge amounts of data to gather invaluable information from it and working with thousands of images is part of our daily workflow. We are dedicated individuals who work as a team with an ultimate goal of deploying these models in user friendly manner. The various projects we have done so far include Music tagging, Maze soler, Automated Check-in system, MRI Segmentation, AI Image Colorization, Cosmic Rays Classification, Plant Leaf Analyser Network and Gesture Interpreter for Specially Abled.
