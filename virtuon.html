VIRTUON

2020-2021




ABSTRACT

We have seen many people buy clothes online and due to this, it is difficult to try clothes and see how they look in those clothes. So here at Drishti we have created a Machine Learning based solution named Virtual Try On Network (VIRTUON) to solve this issue. We require only 2 pictures for cloth transfer one is of the person on which we have to try cloth and another is of person from where cloth is needed to segment.

Model

  • Convolutional Neural Networks(CNNs)
  • Thin Plate Spline Warping Module
  • Open Pose
  • Grapy Module

Workflow

Detect Pose of the person. to find cloth and person in the given pictures. to wrap clothes according to the person. to Fit Cloth onto the person.
